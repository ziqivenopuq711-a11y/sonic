Privacy Policy

How GeniusNews collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: June 1, 2025  |  Effective date: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to GeniusNews. GeniusNews Media ("GeniusNews," "we," "us," or "our") operates the GeniusNews digital media platform, which provides news, analysis, and commentary on cryptocurrency, artificial intelligence, and global financial markets. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our website and interact with our content and services.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data with transparency and care. By accessing or using GeniusNews, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of our platform. We may update this policy periodically; continued use of our platform following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

This policy applies to all information collected through our website, as well as any related services, email newsletters, and communications we provide. It does not apply to third-party websites linked from our platform — we encourage you to review the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several categories of information to provide, maintain, and improve our platform and services:

Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact form submissions (name, email address, message content, subject)
  • Newsletter subscription data (email address, subscription preferences)
  • Press release and editorial tip submissions (name, email, company, submitted content)
  • Advertising and partnership inquiries (name, company, email, contact details)

Information Collected Automatically:

  • Log data: IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, time and date of visit, referring URL, time spent on pages
  • Device information: device type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Usage data: articles read, search queries submitted, navigation patterns
  • Cookie data and similar tracking technologies (see Section 4)

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as financial account numbers, government identification numbers, or health data. We request that you refrain from submitting such information through our contact forms or other input channels.

3. How We Use Your Information

GeniusNews uses the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Platform operation: To provide, maintain, and improve our website, content, and user experience
  • Communications: To respond to your inquiries, process press release submissions, and send newsletters to subscribers who have opted in
  • Analytics: To understand how our audience engages with our content, identify popular topics, and improve our editorial coverage
  • Advertising: To serve relevant advertisements and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns, in compliance with applicable privacy laws
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to fraudulent activity, abuse, or technical issues affecting our platform
  • Legal compliance: To fulfill our obligations under applicable laws and regulations, respond to lawful requests from authorities, and protect our legal rights

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with third-party marketers for their independent marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

4. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

GeniusNews uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our platform and to collect analytics data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us recognize you and remember your preferences across visits.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the basic functionality of our website, including page navigation and security features. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site — which pages are most popular, how users navigate between sections, and where visitors come from. We use this data in aggregated, anonymized form.
  • Advertising cookies: Used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests, based on your browsing behavior on our site and across other sites. These are set by our advertising partners.
  • Preference cookies: Store your settings and preferences (such as display mode or newsletter subscription status) to improve your experience on return visits.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when cookies are set. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Your browser's help documentation provides specific instructions for managing cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

GeniusNews integrates third-party services that may independently collect and process your data according to their own privacy policies. These include:

  • Analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics): We use analytics tools to collect aggregated, anonymized data about site usage. Google's privacy policy governs their data handling.
  • Advertising networks: We work with advertising partners to serve relevant ads on our platform. These partners may use cookies and tracking technologies to build interest profiles and serve targeted advertising.
  • Content delivery networks (CDNs): We use CDNs to deliver website assets efficiently. These services may log request data including IP addresses.
  • Email service providers: Our newsletter delivery is managed through a third-party email platform. Subscriber information is processed according to that provider's privacy policy.
  • Social media platforms: Our pages on social media platforms (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, etc.) are governed by those platforms' privacy policies. We have no control over how those platforms use data you share with them.

We select third-party service providers carefully and require them to maintain appropriate data security standards. However, we are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites or services, even where we have linked to them.

6. Your Rights & Contact

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data. These may include:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations
  • Right to restriction: Request that we limit our processing of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format for transfer to another service
  • Right to object: Object to our processing of your personal data for direct marketing or other purposes based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have questions, concerns, or complaints about our privacy practices, please contact our privacy team:

Email: privacy@geniusnews.io
Company: GeniusNews Media, Digital Publishing Division

We will respond to all privacy-related requests within 30 days. If you are dissatisfied with our response, you may have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority in your jurisdiction.
