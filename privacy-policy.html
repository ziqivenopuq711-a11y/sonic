MonadNews Privacy Policy: Protecting Your Data

Data Collection Practices

MonadNews collects a limited set of personal information necessary to deliver our editorial services and improve the reader experience. When visitors subscribe to our newsletter, we collect an email address used exclusively for newsletter delivery and occasional editorial updates — we do not sell, license, or share subscriber email lists with third parties under any circumstances. Our web analytics infrastructure collects standard anonymized usage data including page views, session duration, referring URLs, and device type information, which we use exclusively for editorial planning and site performance optimization. We do not collect payment information directly — any commercial transactions on our platform are processed through third-party payment providers who maintain their own privacy and security standards. Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to the inquiry and are not added to any marketing lists without explicit consent.

Cookie Usage & User Controls

MonadNews uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to deliver a consistent, functional experience across sessions and to support our analytics infrastructure. Essential cookies maintain basic site functionality including session management and security features — these cannot be disabled without affecting core site performance. Analytics cookies, which track aggregated usage patterns across our editorial content, can be disabled through your browser settings or through opt-out mechanisms provided by our analytics provider. We do not deploy advertising cookies or participate in behavioral advertising networks. Third-party content embedded in our articles — including video players, social media embeds, and interactive data visualizations — may set their own cookies governed by the privacy policies of their respective providers. We encourage readers to review browser cookie controls and to contact us if they have questions about how specific cookies function on our platform.

Your Rights & Contact Information

Readers in jurisdictions covered by privacy regulations including GDPR and CCPA have specific rights regarding their personal data held by MonadNews. These include the right to access the personal data we hold about you, the right to correct inaccurate information, the right to request deletion of your data subject to any legal retention obligations, the right to object to certain processing activities, and the right to data portability in commonly used formats. To exercise any of these rights, submit a written request to our privacy team at community@monadnews.com — we aim to acknowledge all privacy requests within 72 hours and to fulfill requests within the timeframes required by applicable law. MonadNews is committed to handling data rights requests with transparency and without creating unnecessary friction for readers who choose to exercise their legal rights. Our privacy practices are reviewed periodically and updated to reflect changes in applicable law and our operational requirements.
