Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Introduction

AsterNews ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website located at asternews.io (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting the privacy and personal data of every visitor and reader. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you visit our Site, how we use and protect that information, with whom we share it, and what choices you have regarding your data.

By accessing or using AsterNews, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the practices described herein, please discontinue use of the Site. This Policy applies to all information collected through the Site and any related services, communications, or transactions.

We periodically update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, applicable law, or new features of our service. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page, and in certain circumstances, we may provide additional notice such as an on-site notification or email to registered users. We encourage you to review this Policy regularly to stay informed about how we handle your personal information.

2. Data We Collect

We collect several categories of information to operate our Site, improve the reader experience, and fulfil our publishing and business functions. The categories of data we may collect include:

  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other diagnostic data collected automatically when you visit the Site.
  • Device Data: Information about the device you use to access the Site, including hardware model, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Contact Form Data: Name, email address, subject, and message content submitted through our contact form.
  • Newsletter Data: Email address and subscription preferences if you opt in to our newsletters or marketing communications.
  • Cookie Data: Information stored by cookies and similar tracking technologies as described in Section 4 of this Policy.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymized behavioral data processed by third-party analytics providers to help us understand how readers use the Site.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal data such as financial account details, government identification numbers, or health information. If you believe you have inadvertently submitted such information, please contact us immediately at editorial@asternews.io.

3. How We Use Your Data

AsterNews uses the data we collect for the following purposes, each grounded in a lawful basis under applicable data protection law:

Site Operation and Improvement: We use usage data and analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Site, identify technical issues, optimize page performance, and prioritize editorial and product improvements that serve our readers better.

Communications: When you contact us via our contact form or submit a press release, we use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry. If you have opted into newsletters, we use your email address to send editorial content you have requested, and you may unsubscribe at any time.

Security: We process IP addresses and usage data to detect, prevent, and respond to fraudulent activity, denial-of-service attacks, and other security threats to the Site and its infrastructure.

Legal Compliance: We may process and retain certain data as required by applicable law, including for the purposes of satisfying lawful information requests from competent authorities. We will always seek to minimise such disclosures and, where permissible, will notify affected users.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for their marketing purposes. We do not build individual user profiles for targeted advertising, nor do we share data with data brokers.

4. Cookies Policy

AsterNews uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance site functionality, remember your preferences, and collect aggregate analytics data. Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser when you visit a website.

We use the following categories of cookies: Essential cookies that are strictly necessary for the Site to function and cannot be disabled; Analytics cookies that help us understand site usage patterns through aggregated, anonymized data (these may be set by third-party analytics providers operating under data processing agreements with us); and Preference cookies that remember your settings and preferences to improve your experience on return visits.

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies, delete existing cookies, or be notified when a new cookie is set. Please be aware that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of the Site. We do not use advertising cookies or cross-site tracking cookies.

5. Contact & Policy Updates

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact us at editorial@asternews.io. We will endeavour to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within five business days.

You have the right, where applicable under law, to access the personal data we hold about you, to request correction of inaccurate data, to request deletion of your data subject to our legal obligations, and to withdraw consent where processing is based on consent. We are committed to honouring these rights promptly and transparently. This Privacy Policy was last updated on April 30, 2026, and supersedes all previous versions.
