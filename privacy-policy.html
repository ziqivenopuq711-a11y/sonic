Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 April 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to AsterNews. This Privacy Policy describes how AsterNews Media ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards information when you visit our website at asternews.io, subscribe to our newsletter, or otherwise interact with our services. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have questions or concerns about this policy or our practices, please contact us at privacy@asternews.io.

By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. This policy applies to all information collected through our website and related communications, but does not apply to information collected through third-party services we may link to or integrate with.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you subscribe to our newsletter, complete a contact form, create an account, or otherwise communicate with us. This information may include your name, email address, organization affiliation, and the content of any messages you submit. We do not require you to provide personal information simply to read our published articles.

We also collect certain information automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URLs, pages viewed, and the date and time of your visit. This information is collected through standard server logs and analytics tools. We use aggregated and anonymized versions of this data to understand how readers interact with our content and to improve our editorial and technical operations.

Categories of Data We Process

  • Contact information (name, email address) provided voluntarily through forms or subscriptions
  • Technical identifiers (IP address, browser fingerprint, device type) collected automatically
  • Behavioral data (pages visited, time on site, click patterns) collected via analytics tools
  • Communication records (messages sent to editorial or support teams) retained for correspondence purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve our website and editorial services; to send newsletters and editorial updates to subscribers who have opted in; to respond to inquiries, comments, and requests submitted through our contact channels; to monitor and analyze usage patterns for product improvement purposes; and to detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent activity, abuse, or violations of our terms of service.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their direct marketing purposes. We may share aggregated, de-identified statistical information about our readership with advertising partners, but such information cannot be used to identify individual users. Where we engage third-party service providers to assist in delivering our services — such as email delivery platforms, analytics providers, or cloud hosting companies — we ensure that appropriate data processing agreements are in place governing their use of your information.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and to gather analytical data about site usage. Essential cookies are required for the website to function correctly and cannot be disabled. Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our content by collecting and reporting information anonymously. You may configure your browser settings to refuse cookies or to alert you when cookies are being sent. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of some features on our website.

We use Google Analytics to measure website traffic and behavior. Data collected through Google Analytics is processed in accordance with Google's own privacy policy. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by adjusting your browser's privacy settings to block third-party tracking cookies.

5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include the right to access the personal information we hold about you; the right to request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data; the right to request deletion of your personal information subject to certain exceptions; the right to object to or restrict processing of your personal information in certain circumstances; and the right to data portability where technically feasible.

Newsletter subscribers may unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in every email we send. To exercise any other data rights, or to inquire about our data retention practices, please contact us at privacy@asternews.io. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days, subject to verification of your identity. If you believe we have not adequately addressed your privacy concerns, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant supervisory authority in your jurisdiction.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach our data protection officer at privacy@asternews.io, or by writing to AsterNews Media, c/o Privacy Officer. We are committed to working with you to resolve any concerns about the handling of your personal information in a fair and transparent manner.
