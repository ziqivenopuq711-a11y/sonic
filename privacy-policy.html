Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Insiderweb3 ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and safeguards information provided by visitors to our website insiderweb3.com. By using this site, you consent to the data practices outlined in this policy. We are committed to protecting user privacy in accordance with applicable data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

We periodically review and update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and legal requirements. We encourage you to review this page regularly. Continued use of the site following any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect non-personally identifiable information such as browser type, referring pages, operating system, and pages visited when you interact with our site. This data is collected automatically through standard web server logs and analytics tools and is used solely in aggregate form to understand general audience behavior.

We do not require account registration to read our content. Any email addresses voluntarily submitted through our newsletter subscription or contact forms are stored securely and used solely for the purpose for which they were collected — responding to inquiries or delivering subscribed content. We do not enrich or sell this data.

3. Use of Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic patterns, and serve relevant content. Cookies are small text files placed on your device by your browser when you visit our site. They help us remember your preferences and understand how visitors interact with our content over multiple sessions.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time; however, some functionality of the site may be limited or affected as a result. We do not use cookies to track personally identifiable user behavior across third-party websites.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party analytics services such as privacy-respecting, cookieless analytics tools to understand aggregate audience behavior and improve our editorial decisions. These services operate under their own independent privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your explicit consent, except where required to comply with applicable law or protect the rights and safety of Insiderweb3 and its users.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to request deletion of your data, or wish to exercise any other data rights under applicable regulations, please contact us at privacy@insiderweb3.com. We will respond to all verified privacy-related inquiries within 30 days.

For general editorial inquiries, please contact editorial@insiderweb3.com or visit our Contact page.
