CurveNews Privacy Policy: How We Handle Your Data

What Information We Collect

CurveNews collects only the limited information necessary to operate our website and deliver our content services. Newsletter subscribers provide an email address and optionally a name; this information is stored securely and used exclusively for newsletter delivery and related editorial communications. Web server logs record standard technical information including IP addresses, browser type, referring URLs, and page access timestamps, which are retained for 90 days for security and diagnostic purposes and then automatically purged.

We do not collect financial information, cryptocurrency wallet addresses, or trading activity data of any kind. Contact forms on our website collect only the information you voluntarily provide, which is used solely to respond to your specific inquiry and is not retained beyond the resolution of that communication. CurveNews does not purchase, import, or aggregate personal data from third-party data brokers or commercial data providers for any purpose.

Cookies & Tracking Controls

CurveNews uses a minimal set of cookies to support essential website functionality, including session management and user preference storage such as display mode settings. We operate a self-hosted, privacy-respecting analytics platform that does not share data with third parties and automatically anonymizes IP addresses before processing. This approach allows us to understand aggregate reading patterns and content performance without collecting individually identifiable behavioral data about our readers.

We do not use third-party behavioral advertising cookies, cross-site tracking pixels, or user fingerprinting technologies of any kind. Social media sharing buttons on our pages are implemented as simple hyperlinks rather than tracking widgets that report user behavior back to social platforms. Cookie preferences can be managed directly through your browser settings. Disabling non-essential cookies will not affect your ability to read any content on CurveNews or access any of our features.

Your Privacy Rights & How to Contact Us

CurveNews respects and upholds the privacy rights of our readers under applicable regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European residents and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents. You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data CurveNews holds about you. Newsletter subscribers can unsubscribe from all communications at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email or by contacting us directly.

To exercise any of your privacy rights, please contact us at community@curvenews.com with the subject line "Privacy Request." We will acknowledge your request within 72 hours and provide a substantive response within 30 calendar days. CurveNews does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties for marketing, advertising, or commercial purposes under any circumstances. Our business model is built on editorial quality and reader trust, not data monetization.
