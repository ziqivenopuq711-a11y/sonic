How SonicNews Collects and Uses Your Data

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how SonicNews ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you visit SonicNews.io. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. SonicNews is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

This policy is effective as of January 1, 2026, and applies to all users of the SonicNews website and newsletter services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

(a) Information you provide directly — email address when subscribing to our newsletter, name and email when submitting contact forms, and preferences you set on our platform.

(b) Information collected automatically — IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, time and date of visit, referring website, cookies and similar tracking technologies.

(c) Analytics data — via Google Analytics, including aggregate usage patterns, geographic location data (country/city level), and content performance metrics.

We do not collect financial information or cryptocurrency wallet addresses.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to: deliver our newsletter and updates you subscribed to; respond to inquiries and feedback; improve our content and user experience; analyze traffic patterns to understand audience interests; comply with legal obligations; and detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable analytics data with advertising partners for the purpose of understanding our audience demographics.

Cookies Policy

SonicNews uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Essential cookies are required for site functionality and cannot be disabled. Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) help us understand how visitors interact with our content and which articles resonate most with our audience. Marketing cookies may be used to deliver relevant advertising.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or by using our cookie preference center. Disabling non-essential cookies may affect certain site features and personalization options.

Contact & Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data held by SonicNews. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@sonicnews.io.

EU residents have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to data portability, the right to object to processing, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority. California residents have rights under CCPA, including the right to know what personal information we collect and the right to opt out of its sale (note: we do not sell personal data).

Responses to rights requests will be provided within 30 days.
