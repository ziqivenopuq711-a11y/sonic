Stablecoins

U.S. Stablecoin Legislation Advances as Senate Committee Advances Comprehensive Framework Bill

The U.S. Senate Banking Committee has advanced a bipartisan stablecoin regulation bill that would establish clear issuance standards, reserve requirements, and consumer protection obligations for dollar-pegged digital assets. The legislation, considered the most significant crypto regulatory development in years, would create a federal licensing pathway for stablecoin issuers while preserving a role for state-level regulation. Industry observers expect the bill's passage to significantly expand institutional adoption of stablecoins for payments and settlement purposes.
