Avantis AVNT Rewards: 40 Million Tokens Unlocked for XP Holders

Published by AvantisNews Editorial Team — June 2026

The Avantis DeFi protocol has announced a landmark rewards milestone, unlocking a total of 40 million AVNT tokens for distribution to qualified XP holders across its platform. This major token release marks one of the most significant community incentive programs in the protocol's history, rewarding loyal participants who accumulated experience points (XP) through active engagement with Avantis's perpetual trading and liquidity provision features.

XP holders who have consistently contributed to the Avantis ecosystem — whether through trading, staking, or governance participation — are now eligible to claim a proportional share of the 40 million AVNT token pool. The allocation is structured to favour long-term participants, ensuring that those who helped build the protocol's foundation receive the greatest rewards.

What is the AVNT Token?

AVNT is the native governance and utility token of the Avantis protocol, a decentralised finance platform built to support leveraged perpetual trading on multiple blockchain networks. The token grants holders voting rights on protocol upgrades, fee structure changes, and treasury allocations. Additionally, AVNT stakers receive a portion of platform trading fees, creating a direct link between protocol usage and token value.

The token's design incorporates a deflationary mechanism, with a portion of fees used for regular buybacks and burns. This model is intended to reward early and sustained participants while maintaining healthy tokenomics as the platform scales. The current unlock represents approximately 4% of the total AVNT supply, underscoring the protocol's commitment to broad community distribution.

How XP Holders Qualify for Rewards

Experience points on the Avantis platform are accumulated through a variety of on-chain activities. Users earn XP by executing trades on perpetual markets, providing liquidity to trading pools, participating in governance votes, and referring new participants to the platform. The XP system was introduced as a non-transferable measure of engagement, ensuring that rewards flow to genuine contributors rather than passive token holders.

  • Trading on perpetual markets with leverage between 2x and 100x
  • Providing liquidity to the AVNT/USDC and AVNT/ETH pools
  • Casting governance votes on active protocol proposals
  • Completing daily and weekly challenge tasks within the Avantis app
  • Referring active traders who reach minimum volume thresholds

Eligible participants can connect their wallets on the official Avantis rewards portal to verify their XP balance and initiate the claiming process. Claims will remain open for 90 days from the date of the announcement, after which unclaimed tokens will be returned to the community treasury for future distribution events.

Impact on the DeFi Ecosystem

The release of 40 million AVNT tokens into the hands of active community members is expected to have a meaningful impact on both liquidity and governance participation within the Avantis ecosystem. Analysts following the DeFi sector note that large-scale community reward events frequently precede periods of heightened on-chain activity as newly rewarded participants reinvest tokens into protocol features.

Market observers have pointed out that similar community reward programs at other leading DeFi protocols, such as those seen on major derivatives platforms, have driven sustained increases in total value locked (TVL) and daily active users. Avantis's approach of tying rewards to verifiable on-chain contribution, rather than simply holding tokens, is widely regarded as a best practice that aligns incentives between the protocol and its most engaged users.

What Comes Next for Avantis

Following this rewards unlock, the Avantis team has indicated that further token distribution events are planned as part of a multi-phase incentive roadmap extending through 2027. Future phases are expected to introduce new XP categories tied to cross-chain activity, social engagement, and ecosystem partnership participation. The protocol also plans to launch a dedicated governance dashboard that will make it easier for AVNT holders to track active proposals and delegate voting power.

For investors and traders watching the DeFi landscape, Avantis's structured approach to token distribution represents a compelling case study in sustainable community building.
