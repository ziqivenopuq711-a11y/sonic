Avantis (AVNT) Listing: Coinone Unveils Exciting New Trading Opportunity

30 April 2026 Crypto News Tags: AVNT, Coinone, Token Listing, DeFi
AvantisNews Editorial Team

Coinone Announces Official Listing of Avantis (AVNT)

In a move that has generated considerable excitement among the South Korean crypto community, leading digital asset exchange Coinone has announced the official listing of Avantis (AVNT). The new trading pair will go live on May 5, 2026, allowing users to trade AVNT against the Korean Won (AVNT/KRW) directly on the platform. This listing marks a significant milestone for the Avantis project, granting it access to one of Asia's most active and liquid cryptocurrency markets.

Coinone cited Avantis's growing ecosystem and its commitment to transparent, decentralized financial infrastructure as key factors behind the decision. The exchange has a long-standing reputation for rigorous listing reviews, meaning the approval of AVNT signals a strong vote of confidence in the project's fundamentals and long-term viability.

What Is Avantis (AVNT)?

Avantis is a decentralized perpetual trading protocol built on the Base blockchain, designed to offer synthetic asset exposure across a wide range of markets including crypto, forex, and commodities. By leveraging liquidity pools and advanced price oracles, Avantis enables traders to open leveraged positions without the need for centralized counterparties. The AVNT token serves as the protocol's native governance and utility asset, giving holders the ability to vote on key protocol parameters, earn a share of trading fees, and participate in liquidity incentive programs.

Since its launch, Avantis has attracted significant total value locked (TVL) and has positioned itself as a formidable alternative to established perpetuals platforms. Its integration of real-world asset (RWA) price feeds and low-latency execution has resonated strongly with both retail and institutional participants looking for capital-efficient trading solutions on-chain.

Trading Details and Schedule

The Coinone listing will introduce the AVNT/KRW trading pair, with deposit functionality opening on May 3, 2026 — two days ahead of full trading commencement. Withdrawals will be enabled from May 7, 2026 onward, in line with the exchange's standard post-listing cooldown policy. Coinone users are advised to complete KYC verification and ensure their accounts are in good standing before the deposit window opens to avoid any delays in accessing the new market.

The exchange has also noted that AVNT will be eligible for inclusion in several of its reward programs, including trading fee rebates for high-volume users and potential liquidity mining incentives scheduled for the second half of 2026. This added layer of utility is expected to drive meaningful trading volumes in the first weeks following the listing.

Market Implications and Community Reaction

The announcement has been met with enthusiasm across social media platforms and crypto forums, with many participants viewing the Coinone listing as a catalyst for broader adoption of the Avantis protocol. South Korea remains one of the world's largest retail crypto markets by volume, and a listing on a major domestic exchange typically correlates with a notable uptick in both user registrations and on-chain activity for the listed project.

Analysts tracking the DeFi sector have pointed out that the listing could attract a new wave of Korean-speaking traders to the Avantis ecosystem, potentially increasing TVL and bolstering the protocol's on-chain governance participation. Several community members have already begun coordinating liquidity provision campaigns to ensure tight spreads on the new pair from day one.

Key Takeaways

  • Official listing: Avantis (AVNT) will begin trading on Coinone on May 5, 2026, with the AVNT/KRW pair.
  • Deposits open: AVNT deposit functionality activates on May 3, 2026; withdrawals are available from May 7, 2026.
  • Protocol background: Avantis is a decentralized perpetuals trading platform on Base, offering leveraged exposure to crypto, forex, and commodity markets.
  • Token utility: The AVNT token grants governance rights, fee-sharing benefits, and eligibility for liquidity incentives.
  • Market outlook: The Coinone listing is expected to expand Avantis's user base and on-chain activity significantly within the Korean crypto market.
