Advertising & Partnerships

AsterNews offers a range of advertising and sponsorship opportunities for companies seeking to reach a highly qualified audience of crypto traders, DeFi participants, institutional investors, and financial professionals. Our readers are active market participants with above-average disposable income, high digital literacy, and strong purchasing intent for financial products and services.

Available placements include sponsored articles (clearly labelled as such), display advertising across all content verticals, newsletter sponsorships, event co-sponsorship opportunities, and custom content partnerships. We work with advertisers to design campaigns that align with editorial standards and deliver genuine value to our readers — we do not accept advertising that we consider misleading, speculative without basis, or potentially harmful to our audience.

All advertising arrangements are fully disclosed in compliance with applicable press and advertising standards. Sponsored content is clearly labelled as "Sponsored" or "Paid Partnership" and is produced separately from our editorial content pipeline. To receive a media kit and rate card, or to discuss a custom partnership, please contact us at advertising@asternews.io.
