Submit a Press Release

EdgexNews maintains a dedicated press release section where verified industry announcements are published for the benefit of our readership. We accept press release submissions from cryptocurrency projects, blockchain infrastructure companies, DeFi protocols, Web3 startups, financial technology firms, and established institutions operating in the digital asset space. To ensure your press release is processed efficiently and considered for publication, please include the full text of the release in plain text or Microsoft Word format, along with any supporting media assets such as project logos, executive headshots, and infographics in high-resolution format.

All submitted press releases are reviewed by our editorial team before publication. We reserve the right to decline submissions that do not meet our standards for factual accuracy, relevance to our readership, or compliance with our editorial guidelines. Press releases must not make unverifiable claims about token price projections, must clearly identify the issuing organization, and must include verifiable contact information for a named spokesperson who can respond to follow-up questions from our editorial team. Submissions that include complete, accurate information and clearly newsworthy announcements are typically processed and published within 24 to 48 hours of receipt. For expedited publication of time-sensitive announcements, please contact our press team directly via email and mark your submission as urgent.
