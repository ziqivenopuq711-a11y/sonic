Reach Out to MonadNews: Media, Partnerships & Community

Media & Press Inquiries

MonadNews welcomes communication from journalists, communications professionals, and blockchain project teams seeking accurate, thorough coverage of their announcements. Our editorial desk maintains an open channel for embargoed materials and exclusive briefings — project teams can reach our reporters directly to coordinate coverage timing and ensure technical accuracy before publication. We honor embargo agreements and have an established track record of providing fair, technically grounded coverage that serves both the protocol team’s communication goals and our readers’ need for accurate reporting. For all press and media inquiries, contact: press@monadnews.com — we aim to respond within four hours during active market hours.

Partnership Proposals

Organizations seeking to reach MonadNews’s engaged audience of crypto traders, developers, and institutional participants can explore a range of commercial partnership structures. Our readership is concentrated among active market participants: the majority trade crypto regularly, a substantial segment actively use DeFi protocols, and a meaningful portion work professionally as blockchain developers or researchers. Partnership opportunities include newsletter placements, sponsored analytical content clearly labeled as such, and coordinated launch coverage packages for teams releasing new products. All commercial arrangements are disclosed transparently and handled by a dedicated commercial team entirely separate from our editorial operations, preserving the independence of our news coverage. Send partnership proposals to: partners@monadnews.com.

Community Engagement & Feedback

MonadNews is shaped as much by its readers as by its editorial team. We actively invite story tips, factual corrections, topic suggestions, and community feedback through multiple channels. Our Discord server hosts dedicated spaces for technical discussion, trading strategy exchange, and direct conversation with MonadNews reporters and analysts. On Twitter, our @MonadNews account serves as a real-time news channel where community members regularly surface breaking developments that inform our coverage. Guest contributors with genuine domain expertise — protocol developers, security researchers, quantitative traders — are encouraged to pitch original analytical pieces to submit@monadnews.com. For general community questions and feedback, reach us at community@monadnews.com.
