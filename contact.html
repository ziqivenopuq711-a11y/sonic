Contact CurveNews: Media, Partnerships & Reader Engagement

Press & Media Inquiries

CurveNews welcomes inquiries from journalists, broadcasters, and media organizations seeking expert commentary on DeFi markets, stablecoin dynamics, Curve Finance protocol developments, and broader cryptocurrency market trends. Our editorial team is available for on-record interviews, background briefings, and sourcing on breaking crypto market events. Please contact us at press@curvenews.com for all media-related requests.

We respond to media requests within four hours during standard business hours and within twelve hours during evenings and weekends. To ensure the fastest possible response, please include your publication name, submission deadline, and the specific topic area or angle you are covering. Briefings from our team are available via video call or written response depending on deadline requirements and editorial context.

Partnership & Advertising

CurveNews offers commercial partnership opportunities for DeFi protocols, blockchain infrastructure providers, and crypto-native businesses seeking to reach an engaged, technically sophisticated audience. Our readership includes liquidity providers, protocol contributors, institutional DeFi participants, and on-chain researchers who engage deeply with the content we publish. Partnership formats include sponsored research reports, protocol spotlight features, newsletter placements, and event co-sponsorships.

All commercial content is clearly labeled as sponsored and produced separately from editorial operations to maintain the integrity of our independent coverage. Partnership inquiries should be directed to partners@curvenews.com. Please include a brief description of your protocol or product, the audience you aim to reach, and your preferred partnership format. Our partnerships team will respond within two business days with available options and pricing.

Community Feedback & Submissions

CurveNews is committed to building a two-way relationship with our community of readers, researchers, and DeFi practitioners. We welcome story tips, on-chain analysis submissions, and original research contributions from qualified contributors. If you have identified a significant on-chain event, discovered an emerging trend, or produced original DeFi research you believe deserves coverage, please submit to submit@curvenews.com.

General feedback, corrections suggestions, and reader questions can be directed to community@curvenews.com. We read every message and use reader feedback to continuously improve our coverage depth and accuracy. Contributors whose analysis or tips inform published articles are credited by name or handle as preferred. We take editorial collaboration seriously and aim to make CurveNews a platform that reflects the collective intelligence of the DeFi community it serves.
