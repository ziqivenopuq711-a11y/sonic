Contact AvantisNews

Get in Touch

We welcome correspondence from readers, researchers, blockchain developers, and industry professionals. Whether you have a breaking story tip, a press release to distribute, a correction request, or a collaboration proposal, our team is here to help. AvantisNews is committed to open, transparent communication with our community, and we aim to respond to all substantive inquiries within two business days.

Please review the specific contact categories below to ensure your message reaches the right team member as quickly as possible. Providing clear, concise details about your inquiry will help us process your request efficiently and give you the most useful response.

Contact Categories

  • Editorial Inquiries & Story Tips: If you have breaking news, exclusive data, or an industry development you believe our readers should know about, send your tip to our editorial desk. We protect the identity of confidential sources and evaluate all tips based on their public interest value and verifiability.
  • Press Release Submissions: To distribute an official press release to our audience, please provide the full release text, a company contact for verification, and any supporting media assets. Submissions are subject to our editorial review process and compliance guidelines. Note that press release distribution is a paid service; pricing details are available on request.
  • Advertising & Sponsorships: For information about display advertising, newsletter sponsorships, branded content partnerships, or event co-sponsorship opportunities, our commercial team will be happy to provide a media kit and discuss options tailored to your objectives and audience targeting requirements.
  • Corrections & Feedback: If you believe an error has been published, we take accuracy seriously. Please send a detailed correction request specifying the article in question, the factual claim you believe to be incorrect, and supporting evidence. Verified corrections are published prominently alongside the original article.
