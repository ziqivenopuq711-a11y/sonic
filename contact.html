Contact SonicLabsNews: Media, Partnership & Community Inquiries

Press & Media Inquiries: Official Coverage Requests

SonicLabsNews welcomes outreach from journalists, communications professionals, protocol development teams, and public relations agencies seeking authoritative cryptocurrency and blockchain coverage. Our editorial team maintains an open-door policy for press inquiries from credible sources seeking fair, accurate representation of their developments, products, or perspectives in our coverage. Protocol teams launching products or announcing significant developments are encouraged to contact our press desk with embargoed briefing materials, enabling our reporters to prepare comprehensive, technically accurate coverage for publication at the designated embargo lift time.

We prioritize exclusives and embargoed materials from teams whose products represent genuine technical innovation or significant market developments, and we have established track records of honoring embargo agreements and delivering high-quality coverage that serves both the protocol team’s communication objectives and our readers’ need for accurate information. Expert commentary requests — for market analysis, technical explanations of protocol mechanics, or regulatory context — are handled through our dedicated commentary desk, with response times typically under four hours for time-sensitive media requests. Journalists from traditional media outlets seeking background briefings on crypto market dynamics, introductions to protocol teams, or technical explanations of blockchain concepts are welcome to contact our editorial team at press@sonicnewslabs.com.

Partnership & Sponsorship Opportunities

SonicLabsNews offers a range of commercial partnership opportunities for cryptocurrency exchanges, DeFi protocols, infrastructure providers, and service companies seeking to reach our engaged audience of crypto traders, developers, and institutional participants. Our audience metrics position us uniquely at the intersection of technical sophistication and market participation: our readership skews strongly toward active market participants — 78% report making at least one crypto trade per month, 43% self-identify as DeFi users, and 29% are professional developers or technical researchers — making us an exceptionally targeted channel for products and services aimed at serious crypto participants.

Sponsorship packages range from newsletter placement in our daily market briefings, which reach over 85,000 subscribers with above-industry-average open rates, to sponsored analytical content that leverages our editorial team’s expertise to create genuinely valuable educational material that authentically integrates partner messaging. Protocol launch partnerships represent a specialized offering: for projects launching new products or token distributions, we provide coordinated coverage packages that combine editorial content, newsletter distribution, social media amplification, and community engagement to maximize launch visibility within a credible, trusted editorial context. All commercial partnerships are transparently disclosed in accordance with our editorial standards policy. Prospective partners are encouraged to contact our partnerships team at partnerships@sonicnewslabs.com.

Guest Article Submissions: Share Your Expertise

SonicLabsNews actively invites contributions from recognized experts in the cryptocurrency and blockchain space — including professional traders, protocol developers, DeFi researchers, security auditors, regulatory specialists, and macroeconomic analysts — who can contribute original, substantive analysis that enriches our editorial offering. We believe that the best crypto journalism combines professional editorial oversight with domain expertise from practitioners who live and work in the ecosystems they describe, and our guest contributor program is designed to create a structured channel for that expertise to reach our readership.

Submission requirements reflect our commitment to quality: guest articles should be a minimum of 1,500 words, contain original analysis or perspective not available elsewhere, include appropriate citations for factual claims, and be free of undisclosed conflicts of interest. Topics of particular editorial interest include: technical deep-dives on novel protocol mechanics, first-person accounts of DeFi protocol development challenges, rigorous quantitative analysis of on-chain data patterns, comparative analysis of regulatory approaches across jurisdictions, and honest post-mortems of failed crypto projects that offer lessons for the ecosystem. Our editorial review process typically takes 5–7 business days. We compensate quality contributors with a combination of financial payment and significant distribution to our engaged audience. Send submissions to submit@sonicnewslabs.com.

Community Feedback & Reader Engagement

SonicLabsNews is fundamentally a community-driven publication, and we consider reader feedback — including critical feedback about our coverage, corrections to factual errors, and suggestions for topics we should prioritize — to be essential inputs for maintaining the quality and relevance of our editorial product. Our Discord community server serves as the most direct channel for ongoing engagement with our editorial team: dedicated channels allow readers to propose story ideas, discuss ongoing market developments, share on-chain analysis, and engage directly with SonicLabsNews reporters and analysts in real-time conversations.

The community has become an invaluable source of story leads: a substantial percentage of our most impactful investigative pieces originated from tips and observations shared by engaged community members who noticed anomalous on-chain activity, questionable protocol communications, or emerging market developments before they registered on mainstream radar. Our Twitter presence at @SonicLabsNews maintains active engagement with the broader crypto Twitter community, where we participate in ongoing technical discussions, share breaking analysis, and respond to reader questions. For substantive factual corrections, we maintain a formal corrections inbox where readers can submit documented evidence of factual errors in our reporting — all credible correction requests are reviewed within 24 hours and corrections are published prominently alongside the original content when warranted. Community voting on upcoming coverage topics is conducted through periodic Twitter polls and Discord forum votes.

Contact Information & Response Times

We maintain multiple dedicated contact channels to ensure that different types of inquiries reach the appropriate team members and receive timely responses. Press inquiries from journalists, protocol communications teams, and public relations professionals should be directed to press@sonicnewslabs.com — our editorial desk monitors this inbox continuously during market hours and maintains a four-hour response SLA for time-sensitive media requests, with after-hours urgent inquiries typically acknowledged within two hours during active market events. Partnership and sponsorship inquiries should be directed to partnerships@sonicnewslabs.com, where our commercial team will respond within 24 business hours.

Guest article submissions should be sent to submit@sonicnewslabs.com with the full article draft, author bio, and any relevant disclosures — initial editorial assessment responses are provided within 48 business hours. General inquiries including topic suggestions, community questions, and non-urgent reader correspondence can be sent to hello@sonicnewslabs.com. On social media, we maintain active presence on Twitter/X at @SonicLabsNews where direct messages and public mentions are monitored throughout the day. Our Discord community server provides the most immediate response channel for non-confidential inquiries. Our primary office hours for editorial and commercial operations are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM UTC, Monday through Friday, though our news coverage operations run 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to support our commitment to breaking crypto market intelligence without time-zone gaps.
