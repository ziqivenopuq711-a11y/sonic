Reach Our Editorial Team

AstersdexNews welcomes messages from readers, researchers, project teams, and media professionals. Whether you have a news tip about an emerging DeFi protocol, a correction to report on a published article, or an interest in editorial collaboration, our team is committed to responding within 24 business hours.

Please use the contact form to send your inquiry and indicate the subject category most relevant to your message. For time-sensitive security disclosures or market-moving news tips, please mark your submission as urgent and include relevant documentation or on-chain transaction references.

Partnership & Advertising Enquiries

AstersdexNews maintains a strict separation between editorial and commercial operations. However, we do work with reputable crypto projects, exchanges, and infrastructure providers on clearly labelled sponsored content, banner advertising, and event partnerships.

All commercial enquiries are reviewed by our partnerships team and must meet our editorial standards for factual accuracy and transparency. Project teams seeking coverage should note that we do not accept payment for editorial placement — all news coverage decisions are made independently by our editorial team based on newsworthiness.

Response time: All enquiries are reviewed within 24 business hours. Urgent news tips are prioritised and reviewed within 4 hours during trading hours.
