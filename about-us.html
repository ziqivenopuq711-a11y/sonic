About AsterNews

Who We Are

AsterNews was founded in 2022 with a clear and focused mission: to become the most trusted independent media platform for digital asset and financial market news. Born at the intersection of the crypto industry's maturation and the growing convergence of traditional finance with blockchain-based technologies, AsterNews was built by a team of journalists, analysts, and technologists who believed that the space deserved a publication that prioritized accuracy, depth, and editorial independence above all else.

Since our founding, we have grown from a small editorial team covering Bitcoin and Ethereum into a comprehensive financial news operation with dedicated verticals for cryptocurrency markets, artificial intelligence in finance, and foreign exchange markets. Our readership spans more than 140 countries, with a core audience of traders, investors, developers, and financial professionals who rely on AsterNews as a primary daily source of market intelligence.

We cover the stories that matter: protocol upgrades and their market implications, macroeconomic events and their ripple effects across digital assets, regulatory developments in major jurisdictions, and the technological breakthroughs that are reshaping what is possible in decentralized finance. Our reporting is original, our analysis is independent, and our commitment to getting the facts right is absolute. We are not affiliated with any exchange, token project, or financial services firm, and we do not allow commercial relationships to influence our editorial coverage.

Our Mission

AsterNews exists to democratize access to high-quality financial journalism in the digital asset era. Too often, crypto media has been dominated by promotional content dressed as news, by sponsored articles written to drive token purchases, and by commentary that serves the interests of the industry rather than its participants. We reject that model entirely.

Our mission is to provide every reader — whether a seasoned institutional trader or someone exploring crypto for the first time — with the same quality of unbiased, rigorously researched, and clearly written journalism that was once available only to subscribers of elite financial publications. We believe an informed market is a healthier market, and that honest reporting serves both individual readers and the long-term credibility of the digital asset ecosystem as a whole.

Every editorial decision at AsterNews is guided by three principles: accuracy first, context always, and independence never compromised. We correct our mistakes promptly, we disclose any potential conflicts of interest transparently, and we hold ourselves to the same journalistic standards as the most respected financial publications in the world.

Why Choose AsterNews

  • Independent Editorial: We have no financial relationships with the projects or companies we cover. Our coverage is driven by newsworthiness, not commercial interest.
  • Expert Analysis: Our editorial team includes former quantitative analysts, blockchain developers, and economists who bring genuine domain expertise to every story.
  • Comprehensive Coverage: From Bitcoin price action to ECB policy decisions to the latest AI research — we cover the full financial ecosystem that intersects with digital assets.
  • Real-Time Updates: Our newsroom operates 24/7 to ensure that breaking developments in global markets are covered the moment they occur, regardless of time zone.
  • Reader Trust: With over 850,000 monthly unique visitors and a Net Promoter Score consistently above 65, AsterNews has built genuine reader loyalty grounded in consistent quality and reliability.

Our Team

AsterNews is built on the talent and dedication of its editorial team. Our journalists bring backgrounds from traditional financial media, fintech startups, academic research, and quantitative trading — a diverse mix of perspectives that enriches our coverage and prevents the groupthink that can afflict specialist publications.

Our Editor-in-Chief oversees editorial standards and strategic direction, supported by vertical editors responsible for Crypto, AI & Technology, and Forex coverage respectively. A team of senior reporters, staff writers, and contributing analysts produces our daily content, while a dedicated research and data team maintains the on-chain analytics and market data infrastructure that underpins our quantitative reporting. We are a distributed team operating across multiple time zones, ensuring that AsterNews is always awake when the markets are.
