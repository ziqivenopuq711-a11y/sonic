About AsterNews

Independent crypto and financial media since 2024

Who We Are

AsterNews is a digital media platform founded in 2024 with a clear mission: to provide independent, data-driven, and accessible coverage of the cryptocurrency markets, blockchain ecosystem, artificial intelligence developments, and global forex trends. In an industry saturated with hype, we cut through the noise to deliver journalism that informs rather than sensationalizes. Our editorial team comprises seasoned reporters, on-chain analysts, and financial professionals who share a commitment to accuracy and transparency.

We launched AsterNews because we saw a gap in crypto media — too many outlets chasing engagement metrics at the expense of editorial integrity. From our first day, we established strict policies against sponsored editorial content, pay-to-publish arrangements, and undisclosed conflicts of interest. Every story on AsterNews is produced independently and held to the same journalistic standards as any leading financial publication.

What We Cover

Our coverage spans the full breadth of digital finance. On the crypto side, we track Bitcoin and altcoin market movements, DeFi protocol developments, NFT ecosystem trends, regulatory updates across major jurisdictions, and the tokenomics decisions that shape project trajectories. Our AI news desk monitors the intersection of machine learning, large language models, and their growing influence on trading, financial analysis, and blockchain infrastructure. Our forex team provides real-time analysis of major currency pairs, central bank decisions, and macroeconomic drivers.

Our Coverage Areas

  • Bitcoin & major altcoin markets — price analysis, on-chain metrics, and sentiment data
  • DeFi protocols — governance, tokenomics, liquidity, and security audits
  • Artificial intelligence in finance — trading bots, predictive models, and AI-driven analytics
  • Forex markets — G10 currency pairs, EM currencies, and central bank policy
  • Regulatory developments — global crypto legislation, enforcement actions, and compliance

Our Editorial Standards

Every article published on AsterNews goes through a multi-stage editorial review process. Reporters are required to cite primary sources, on-chain data providers, or verified documentation for all factual claims. Opinion pieces are clearly labeled and kept separate from news reporting. We operate a corrections policy that requires any material factual error to be publicly acknowledged and corrected within 24 hours of identification. Our goal is not to be the fastest outlet — it is to be the most reliable one.

We also maintain a strict conflict-of-interest policy. No AsterNews editorial staff member is permitted to hold undisclosed positions in assets covered in their reporting. Any staff member who holds a relevant position must disclose it at the end of any article covering that asset. These standards are audited quarterly by our editorial oversight committee.

Join Our Community

AsterNews is more than a news outlet — it is a growing community of crypto-curious readers, professional traders, developers, and researchers who rely on quality information to make better decisions.
