About GeniusNews

Independent intelligence for the decentralized world.

Who We Are

GeniusNews was founded in 2022 by a team of financial journalists, blockchain developers, and digital media veterans united by a single conviction: that the emerging world of cryptocurrency, decentralized finance, and artificial intelligence deserved reporting that was rigorous, independent, and genuinely useful to readers. Since our inception, we have grown into a globally recognized digital media platform serving hundreds of thousands of readers across more than 80 countries.

Our editorial team comprises experienced professionals with backgrounds spanning traditional finance, blockchain engineering, investigative journalism, and data science. This multidisciplinary approach allows GeniusNews to go beyond surface-level price reporting and deliver the kind of substantive, context-rich analysis that sophisticated market participants need to make informed decisions. We are proud to operate free from the influence of any single project, protocol, or financial interest, maintaining the editorial independence that our readers depend on.

Headquartered in the digital-first publishing space, GeniusNews operates with a globally distributed team of writers, analysts, and editors who work across time zones to ensure our coverage never sleeps. From breaking news alerts to in-depth feature investigations, our commitment is always to accuracy, clarity, and depth.

Our Mission & Vision

Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality financial and technology intelligence. In a world where information asymmetry drives market outcomes, we believe that every investor — regardless of their background or the size of their portfolio — deserves access to the same caliber of analysis previously reserved for institutional desks and hedge funds. GeniusNews exists to close that gap.

Our vision extends beyond being a news outlet. We aspire to be the definitive intelligence layer for the digital economy — a trusted resource that readers return to not merely to learn what happened, but to understand why it happened, what it means for the broader ecosystem, and what is likely to come next. We are building toward a future where financial literacy in crypto, AI, and global markets is universally accessible, and where our platform serves as the foundation upon which that literacy is built.

We hold ourselves to the highest standards of journalistic integrity, fact-checking every claim, crediting every source, and issuing corrections promptly when errors occur. Our readers' trust is the most valuable asset we possess, and we work every day to earn and protect it.

What We Cover

GeniusNews provides comprehensive coverage across the full spectrum of the digital finance ecosystem. Our core coverage areas include cryptocurrency markets — from Bitcoin and Ethereum to emerging altcoins, DeFi protocols, and NFT ecosystems — as well as the rapidly evolving intersection of artificial intelligence and financial technology. We track AI developments in trading, blockchain security, predictive analytics, and the regulatory frameworks being constructed around these technologies.

Beyond crypto, our forex desk monitors global currency markets with a particular focus on how macroeconomic events — central bank decisions, geopolitical shifts, inflation data — interact with the cryptocurrency markets that increasingly move in tandem with traditional finance. Our press release section provides a vetted channel for projects and organizations to communicate directly with our readership, while our investigative features dig deeper into the stories that define the industry's direction.

Bitcoin, Ethereum, altcoins, decentralized exchanges, yield farming, liquidity protocols, and tokenomics analysis.

Artificial Intelligence

AI in trading, blockchain security, machine learning models, generative AI, and regulatory developments.

Why Trust GeniusNews

Trust in media is not granted — it is earned through consistent, verifiable behavior over time. At GeniusNews, we have built our credibility on a foundation of transparent sourcing, rigorous editorial standards, and an unwavering commitment to our readers over any commercial consideration. We clearly label sponsored content and press releases, ensuring our editorial voice is never confused with paid promotion. Our journalists are encouraged to challenge prevailing narratives, question project claims, and follow evidence wherever it leads.

We have been cited by leading financial publications, quoted by policymakers, and referenced in academic research. Our accuracy rate on market forecasts and regulatory predictions is among the highest in digital crypto media, a testament to the quality of our analytical team and their dedication to getting the story right. When we make a mistake — as all publications occasionally do — we correct it immediately and publicly, without equivocation.

Our readers include professional traders, institutional investors, blockchain developers, policy researchers, and curious newcomers to the digital economy. Each of them matters equally to us, and we craft our content to be accessible to newcomers while remaining substantive enough to satisfy seasoned professionals. That balance is the hallmark of the GeniusNews editorial philosophy.
