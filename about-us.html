About AstersdexNews

Independent crypto & financial market media since 2024

Who We Are

AstersdexNews is an independent digital media company founded in 2024 with a mission to deliver accurate, timely, and insightful coverage of the cryptocurrency and decentralized finance landscape. Our editorial team comprises seasoned journalists, on-chain analysts, and former fintech professionals who bring a rigorous, data-driven perspective to every story we publish.

We believe that informed readers make better decisions, and our commitment to editorial independence ensures that our coverage is never influenced by advertiser or project relationships. From breaking protocol exploits to in-depth tokenomics analyses, AstersdexNews holds itself to the highest standards of financial journalism in the digital asset space.

What We Cover

Our coverage spans five core verticals: cryptocurrency market news, DeFi and Web3 protocol updates, artificial intelligence developments intersecting with blockchain, traditional forex and macro market analysis, and regulatory affairs affecting digital assets globally.

Whether you are a retail investor tracking the latest Bitcoin price movements or an institutional analyst researching emerging Layer-2 ecosystems, AstersdexNews provides the depth and breadth of coverage you need to stay ahead of the market. We publish original research, exclusive interviews, and daily market roundups across all our content channels, seven days a week.

Our Editorial Standards

Every article published on AstersdexNews undergoes a multi-step editorial review process. Claims are verified against on-chain data, official project announcements, and reputable secondary sources before publication. We clearly distinguish between news reporting and opinion commentary, and we proactively issue corrections when errors are identified.

Our advertising policy prohibits sponsored content from being presented as editorial news, and all commercial partnerships are disclosed prominently. We are committed to building the most trustworthy crypto news destination on the internet — a resource that the DeFi community can rely on when it matters most: during market-moving events, protocol crises, and regulatory inflection points.

Editorial independence: AstersdexNews accepts no payment for editorial coverage decisions. Our newsroom operates on a strict separation between commercial and editorial functions.
