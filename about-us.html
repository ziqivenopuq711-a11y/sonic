About AvantisNews

Independent journalism at the intersection of crypto, AI, and global finance.

Who We Are

AvantisNews is an independent digital media outlet dedicated to delivering timely, accurate, and in-depth reporting on cryptocurrency markets, blockchain technology, artificial intelligence developments, and global forex trends. Founded by a team of financial journalists, blockchain engineers, and data analysts, AvantisNews was built on the belief that readers deserve rigorous, conflict-free coverage of the most transformative technologies reshaping the global economy.

We serve a diverse global audience ranging from seasoned institutional traders and DeFi developers to curious newcomers exploring digital assets for the first time. Our editorial team combines deep technical expertise with clear, accessible writing to ensure that complex concepts are communicated without sacrificing accuracy or depth.

Our Mission

Our mission is straightforward: to be the most trusted source of intelligence for those navigating the rapidly evolving worlds of cryptocurrency, AI, and forex. We pursue this mission by maintaining strict editorial independence, verifying facts before publication, and providing analysis that goes beyond surface-level price reporting to explore the underlying forces driving market movements and technological shifts.

AvantisNews does not accept payment for editorial coverage. Our revenue comes entirely from display advertising and clearly labelled sponsored content, which is always separated from our independent journalism. We believe that transparency about how we operate is fundamental to earning and maintaining reader trust.

Our Coverage Areas

  • Cryptocurrency Markets: Breaking news, price analysis, Bitcoin and altcoin developments, and exchange updates.
  • Blockchain Technology: Protocol upgrades, DeFi innovation, NFT markets, and Layer 2 scaling solutions.
  • Artificial Intelligence: AI applications in trading, blockchain security, predictive analytics, and generative AI tools.
  • Forex & Macro Finance: Central bank policy, currency correlations, crypto-forex intersections, and global market outlooks.
  • Press Releases: Official announcements from leading blockchain projects, exchanges, and technology firms.

Our Values

Integrity, accuracy, and accessibility are the core values that guide everything we do at AvantisNews. We hold ourselves to the highest journalistic standards, correcting errors promptly and transparently when they occur. We actively seek out diverse perspectives from across the global crypto and finance community, ensuring our coverage reflects a wide range of viewpoints and expertise.

We are committed to making financial and technological knowledge accessible to everyone, regardless of background or experience level. Through clear explainers, market analysis, and investigative reporting, we empower our readers to make informed decisions in an increasingly complex digital financial landscape.
