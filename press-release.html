Aster DEX Announces Strategic Partnership With Three Major Market Makers

April 30, 2026

Aster DEX today announced the formation of strategic liquidity partnerships with three of the top-ten cryptocurrency market makers by volume: Apex Liquidity Partners, QuantNode Capital, and StellarMM. Under the agreements, all three firms will deploy dedicated liquidity into Aster DEX's order-book module at launch of the Zero-Fee Epoch, providing continuous two-sided quotes across the protocol's top 30 trading pairs. The partnerships are structured as multi-year arrangements with performance-based incentive tiers tied to spread quality and uptime metrics. Aster DEX's CEO described the deals as a pivotal step in establishing the protocol as the most liquid decentralized exchange in operation and stated that additional market-maker partnerships are expected to be announced before the end of Q2 2026.

CryptoVault Raises $80M Series B to Expand Institutional Custody Services

April 26, 2026

CryptoVault, a leading regulated digital asset custodian, has closed an $80 million Series B funding round co-led by Northbridge Ventures and Pacific Digital Growth Fund, with participation from three major bank-affiliated investment arms. The capital will be used to accelerate product development across CryptoVault's multi-signature custody infrastructure, expand its regulated entity footprint to five new jurisdictions including Japan, Singapore, and the United Arab Emirates, and build out a new staking-as-a-service offering that will allow institutional clients to earn yield on proof-of-stake assets held in qualified custody. The raise values CryptoVault at approximately $620 million post-money, making it one of the most highly valued pure-play custody companies in the digital asset space.

ChainBridge Protocol Achieves SOC 2 Type II Certification for Cross-Chain Transfers

April 20, 2026

ChainBridge Protocol announced today that it has successfully completed a SOC 2 Type II audit covering its cross-chain message passing and asset transfer infrastructure, becoming the first decentralized bridge protocol to achieve this certification. The audit, conducted by independent third-party firm CipherAudit, evaluated ChainBridge's systems and controls over a six-month observation period, assessing security, availability, and confidentiality against the American Institute of Certified Public Accountants' Trust Services Criteria. The achievement is expected to accelerate enterprise adoption of ChainBridge's interoperability stack, as many institutional partners and regulated financial entities require SOC 2 compliance from technology vendors before engaging with decentralized infrastructure products.
