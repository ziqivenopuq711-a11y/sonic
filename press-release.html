Aster Foundation Announces Public Testnet Launch and Aster Surge Incentive Program

The Aster Foundation today officially announced the activation of the Aster Chain public testnet and the simultaneous launch of the Aster Surge incentive program, which allocates 5 million ASTER tokens as rewards for validators, developers, and community members who contribute to the testnet phase. The foundation confirmed that mainnet launch remains targeted for Q3 2026, contingent on a minimum eight-week period of stable testnet operation without critical protocol-level issues.
