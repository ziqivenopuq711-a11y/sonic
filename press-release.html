Aster DEX Announces Integration with LayerZero for Cross-Chain Perpetuals

DeFi

Aster DEX has entered into a technical integration agreement with LayerZero, the interoperability protocol, enabling cross-chain perpetual futures trading across Ethereum, Arbitrum, and Solana networks from a single unified interface. The integration is expected to go live in Q3 2026 and will allow traders to maintain a single margin account while accessing liquidity pools across three major blockchain ecosystems simultaneously.
