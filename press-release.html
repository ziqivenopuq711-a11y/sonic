AsterNews accepts press release submissions from verified blockchain projects, registered financial technology companies, and publicly accountable institutions. All submissions are reviewed by our editorial team for factual accuracy, completeness, and newsworthiness before publication. Submission to AsterNews does not guarantee publication. We do not accept payment in exchange for editorial placement. To submit a press release, email our press team at press@asternews.io with the subject line "Press Release Submission." Include the full release text, supporting documentation, official contact information, and any embargo instructions in your initial email. Submissions received before 10:00 AM UTC on weekdays are eligible for same-day editorial review.

Featured: Aster DEX Announces Revised Tokenomics Framework

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — April 29, 2026

Aster DEX, a leading decentralized exchange protocol operating on Ethereum and compatible Layer-2 networks, today announced the implementation of a revised tokenomics framework that reduces monthly token unlock volumes by 97%. The announcement follows a governance vote that passed with 94% approval from token holders who participated in the two-week voting window.

Under the new framework, monthly token unlocks will be capped at 3.6 million tokens, down from the previous maximum of 120 million tokens per month. Team and founding contributor allocations will now vest over a 48-month linear schedule with a mandatory 12-month cliff, while early investor tranches will transition to quarterly distribution over a 36-month period. The protocol will simultaneously deploy a dynamic throttle mechanism capable of further reducing unlock rates if on-chain price impact parameters exceed preset thresholds.

"This decision reflects our community's clear mandate for long-term protocol health over short-term liquidity. We are committed to building the tokenomics model that sustainable DeFi infrastructure deserves." — Aster DEX Core Contributors

The revised tokenomics will take effect at the beginning of May 2026. A full technical specification document and implementation timeline have been published on the Aster DEX governance forum. Token holders seeking additional details on the vesting schedule changes are encouraged to consult the official forum thread or contact the protocol's community support channel.
