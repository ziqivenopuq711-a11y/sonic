Featured Press Release: Sonic Labs & Immunefi Launch $2M Bug Bounty

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE

December 31, 2024

SONIC LABS AND IMMUNEFI LAUNCH $2 MILLION BUG BOUNTY PROGRAM TO FORTIFY BLOCKCHAIN SECURITY

Sonic Labs, the development team behind the Sonic high-performance blockchain (formerly Fantom), today announced the launch of a $2 million bug bounty program in partnership with Immunefi, the leading Web3 security platform. The program targets vulnerabilities in both the Sonic L1 protocol and the Gateway bridge — the cross-chain infrastructure connecting Sonic to the Ethereum network and enabling the transfer of EVM-compatible assets including USDC and Wrapped Ether.

The bug bounty program follows comprehensive security audits conducted by OpenZeppelin, Quantstamp, and Certora. By partnering with Immunefi, Sonic Labs taps into a global community of security researchers and white-hat hackers who have collectively identified and responsibly disclosed vulnerabilities worth over $110 million across Web3 protocols.

The program reflects Sonic Labs' commitment to building a secure, trustworthy blockchain infrastructure as the network prepares for broader DeFi adoption and increased total value locked. Exchanges including Binance support the ongoing 1:1 Fantom-to-Sonic token migration, underscoring broad industry confidence in the Sonic ecosystem.
