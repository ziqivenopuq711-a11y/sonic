NexusFinance Launches Mainnet With $50M TVL on Day One, Pioneering Omnichain Yield Aggregation

NexusFinance has officially launched its mainnet protocol, attracting over $50 million in total value locked within the first 24 hours of operation. VELA Token Secures Tier-1 Exchange Listings on Three Major Platforms Simultaneously

The VELA governance token has announced simultaneous listing agreements with three top-10 cryptocurrency exchanges by trading volume, marking one of the most significant multi-exchange launches of the quarter. The coordinated listing strategy is designed to maximize initial liquidity depth and ensure broad accessibility for retail and institutional investors from day one. Partnership

Chainlink and ArcadeNet Announce Strategic Integration to Power Web3 Gaming Data Infrastructure

Chainlink Labs and ArcadeNet have announced a strategic partnership to integrate Chainlink's decentralized oracle network directly into ArcadeNet's Web3 gaming infrastructure, enabling verifiable random number generation, real-world data feeds, and cross-chain NFT interoperability for all games deployed on the ArcadeNet platform. The integration is expected to benefit over 200 gaming studios currently building on the network.

NFT Marketplace

OpenCanvas NFT Marketplace Introduces Creator Royalty Enforcement at the Protocol Level

OpenCanvas, a rapidly growing NFT marketplace, has announced the implementation of protocol-level creator royalty enforcement, becoming one of the first major platforms to guarantee that artists and creators receive their designated royalty percentage on every secondary sale regardless of which marketplace the transaction occurs on. The solution leverages a novel on-chain royalty registry that is compatible with major NFT standards and cannot be bypassed by royalty-free marketplaces.

June 8, 2025  |  Press Release

Tokenomics

PulsarDAO Completes Governance-Approved Tokenomics Overhaul to Enhance Long-Term Sustainability

PulsarDAO has successfully executed a community-approved tokenomics revision that reduces maximum token supply by 30%, increases the portion allocated to the protocol treasury from 10% to 25%, and introduces a new deflationary burn mechanism tied to protocol revenue. The changes, ratified by over 82% of participating governance token holders, are designed to create a more sustainable economic model and align incentives between long-term holders and the protocol's growth objectives.

June 6, 2025  |  Press Release

Web3 Infrastructure

GridNode Raises $28M Series A to Scale Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Network Across Asia

GridNode, a decentralized physical infrastructure network (DePIN) project focused on distributed computing and wireless connectivity, has closed a $28 million Series A funding round led by two prominent Web3-focused venture funds. The capital will be deployed to expand GridNode's node operator network across Southeast Asia, onboard enterprise customers seeking decentralized cloud computing alternatives, and accelerate development of the protocol's token-incentivized contribution model.
