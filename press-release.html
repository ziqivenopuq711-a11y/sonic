Verified press releases and official communications from blockchain projects, exchanges, and technology companies.

How to Submit a Press Release

AvantisNews accepts press release submissions from blockchain projects, cryptocurrency exchanges, DeFi protocols, AI companies, and financial technology firms. Our editorial team reviews all submissions to verify factual claims and ensure compliance with our publication standards. Approved releases are published promptly with clear labelling to distinguish them from independent editorial content.

To submit a press release, email your complete release document along with supporting materials to press@avantisnews.com. Please include a media contact name, phone number, and email address to facilitate follow-up questions from our editorial team. We aim to acknowledge all submissions within 24 hours and publish approved releases within 48 hours of final approval.

Recent Blockchain Partnership Announcements

The blockchain sector continues to see a surge in strategic partnerships as established financial institutions, technology conglomerates, and government entities explore blockchain integration. Recent announcements have included collaborations between major payment networks and Layer 1 blockchain protocols to streamline cross-border settlement, as well as agreements between enterprise software companies and public blockchains to bring supply chain transparency to global logistics networks.

  • Global payment networks integrating stablecoin settlement layers
  • Major cloud providers launching blockchain-as-a-service infrastructure
  • Insurance companies piloting on-chain parametric insurance products
  • Governments issuing digital bonds on public blockchain networks

These partnerships signal the deepening integration of blockchain technology into mainstream commercial and governmental operations, moving beyond speculative positioning toward concrete operational deployments with measurable efficiency gains.

Major Protocol Upgrades and Network Milestones

Protocol upgrade announcements represent some of the most technically significant press releases in the blockchain space. These communications detail changes to network consensus mechanisms, transaction throughput improvements, fee market redesigns, and the introduction of new smart contract capabilities. AvantisNews provides technical summaries alongside official release text to help both technical and non-technical readers understand the implications of major upgrades.

Network milestone announcements — covering events such as the processing of one billion cumulative transactions, reaching new TVL records, or achieving cross-chain interoperability goals — are similarly covered in our press release section. These milestones provide important context for evaluating the real-world adoption and technical maturity of blockchain protocols.

Exchange Listings and Token Launch Announcements

Exchange listing announcements remain among the most impactful events in a token's lifecycle, typically driving significant increases in trading volume, price discovery, and market visibility. AvantisNews covers listing announcements from both major centralised exchanges and leading decentralised platforms, providing context on the projects involved, listing conditions, and market implications.

Token launch announcements, including initial DEX offerings, public sales, and airdrop distributions, are also covered in our press release section. We strongly encourage readers to conduct independent due diligence on all token projects before participating in any sale or distribution event.
