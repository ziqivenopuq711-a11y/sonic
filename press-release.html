NovaDeFi Protocol Launches $50M Ecosystem Development Fund on Arbitrum

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — April 30, 2026

NovaDeFi Protocol today announced the creation of a $50 million Ecosystem Development Fund (EDF) to accelerate the growth of the NovaDeFi network on Arbitrum One. The fund will be deployed over a 24-month period to support developers building decentralized applications, liquidity providers contributing to core protocol pools, and community initiatives that advance the long-term health of the ecosystem. The first tranche of $12 million will be allocated through a transparent on-chain grant program with proposals open for community voting beginning May 15, 2026. NovaDeFi Chief Executive Officer Marcus Chen stated: "This fund represents our most significant commitment to ecosystem growth to date. We are inviting builders, researchers, and liquidity partners from across the DeFi community to shape the next chapter of NovaDeFi together."

VaultChain Secures $30M Series B to Expand Institutional Crypto Custody

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — April 28, 2026

VaultChain, a regulated digital asset custody and settlement infrastructure provider, has successfully closed a $30 million Series B funding round led by Horizon Capital Partners, with participation from several notable institutional investors including a sovereign wealth fund and two major European asset managers. The proceeds will be used to expand VaultChain's operational capacity across Asia-Pacific markets, enhance its multi-party computation (MPC) wallet infrastructure, and accelerate the development of its tokenized real-world asset settlement layer. The round brings VaultChain's total funding to $52 million and positions the company for a planned regulatory licensing campaign across six additional jurisdictions in 2026. CEO Priya Sharma commented: "Institutional demand for compliant, auditable crypto custody solutions has never been stronger. This round gives us the resources to meet that demand at scale."

Meridian Exchange Introduces Zero-Fee Perpetuals Trading for Verified Users

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE — April 25, 2026

Meridian Exchange, a centralized derivatives trading platform with over two million registered users, today announced the launch of its Zero-Fee Perpetuals program, which will eliminate maker and taker fees on perpetual futures contracts for all users who complete advanced verification and maintain a minimum account balance of $1,000 in USDC. The program, initially available for BTC, ETH, and SOL perpetual pairs, is intended to compete directly with decentralized perpetual protocols that have captured significant market share through their fee structures. Meridian will fund the program through a combination of spread capture, liquidation engine revenues, and returns from its treasury management operations. The exchange has confirmed plans to expand the zero-fee program to additional trading pairs by Q3 2026 contingent on volume targets being met.
