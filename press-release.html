Lombard Finance Launches LBTC Token and $250,000 Security Bounty Program

FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE • April 28, 2026 • Dubai, UAE

Lombard Finance today announced the official launch of LBTC, a liquid Bitcoin token designed to enable productive deployment of Bitcoin capital across the decentralized finance ecosystem. Simultaneously, the protocol has activated a $250,000 bug bounty program administered through an independent blockchain security platform, inviting the global researcher community to identify and responsibly disclose vulnerabilities in exchange for substantial financial rewards. The protocol has also announced a live integration with the Morpho lending and borrowing ecosystem, which collectively manages over $2 billion in digital assets.

Morpho Protocol Announces $500M Expansion of Institutional Lending Vaults

Morpho Protocol has unveiled a major expansion of its institutional lending infrastructure, adding $500 million in new vault capacity specifically designed to accommodate large-scale capital deployments from regulated entities. The expansion includes dedicated vaults for Bitcoin-backed collateral, expanding access for entities holding LBTC, WBTC, and other liquid Bitcoin derivatives. The announcement is accompanied by the introduction of enhanced risk management tooling built in partnership with leading on-chain analytics providers, giving institutional lenders granular visibility into counterparty exposure and collateral health in real time.

Leading Blockchain Security Firm Expands Bug Bounty Marketplace to Web3 Protocols

A top-tier blockchain security company has announced the expansion of its bug bounty marketplace, onboarding over thirty new Web3 protocols in a single month and growing total bounty pool value past $50 million. The platform facilitates structured vulnerability disclosure between independent security researchers and protocol development teams, offering on-chain payment settlement, reputation-based researcher profiles, and third-party arbitration for disputed findings. The initiative addresses a persistent challenge in the decentralized space: the gap between the speed of protocol deployment and the thoroughness of security verification.

Real-World Asset Tokenization Platform Reaches $10BN in Tokenized Assets

A pioneering real-world asset tokenization platform has announced that the aggregate value of tokenized assets hosted on its infrastructure has crossed $10 billion, encompassing private credit instruments, real estate portfolios, Treasury bills, and infrastructure project bonds. The milestone reflects accelerating institutional interest in blockchain-based asset administration, with several sovereign wealth funds and major investment banks having completed pilot programs and moved to full production deployment.
