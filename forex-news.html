USD Weakens as Federal Reserve Signals Potential Rate Cuts in Q3 2026

April 29, 2026

The US Dollar Index (DXY) fell 1.4% over the past week after Federal Reserve Chair Jerome Powell delivered remarks at the Jackson Hole symposium that markets interpreted as a clear signal of an imminent rate-cutting cycle. Powell cited moderation in core inflation, a softening labour market, and easing financial conditions as justification for a more accommodative policy stance in the second half of 2026. Futures markets now price in a 78% probability of at least two 25-basis-point cuts before year end. The dollar's weakness has broadly benefited risk assets, with gold, equities, and cryptocurrencies all posting gains in the aftermath of the announcement.

EUR/USD Tests Key Resistance as European Growth Data Beats Forecasts

April 24, 2026

The EUR/USD currency pair pushed above the 1.1200 level this week, testing a resistance zone that has capped rallies on four separate occasions since January, after a batch of stronger-than-expected economic data from the Eurozone reinforced the case for continued ECB policy divergence from the Fed. Germany's GDP growth came in at 0.7% quarter-on-quarter, beating the consensus estimate of 0.4%, while eurozone-wide PMI data showed the manufacturing sector entering expansionary territory for the first time in over a year. Technical analysts now identify 1.1350 as the next meaningful resistance level if bulls can sustain a weekly close above the current threshold.

Crypto Stablecoins Increasingly Used as Forex Hedge by Emerging Markets

April 19, 2026

A growing body of on-chain and survey data confirms that USD-pegged stablecoins are increasingly being used as a practical foreign exchange hedge by individuals and small businesses in emerging market economies experiencing local currency volatility. In countries where the domestic currency has depreciated more than 20% against the dollar in the past 12 months, stablecoin adoption has accelerated markedly, with peer-to-peer stablecoin transaction volumes surging. Central banks in several affected nations have responded with regulatory frameworks that attempt to balance financial inclusion benefits against concerns about currency substitution and the erosion of monetary policy effectiveness, creating a complex evolving landscape for stablecoin operators and users alike.

About Forex & Currency Markets Coverage at AsterNews

The global foreign exchange market remains the largest and most liquid financial market in the world, with daily trading volumes exceeding $7 trillion. Our forex coverage is built on the recognition that currency markets do not exist in isolation — they are deeply interconnected with interest rate policy, inflation dynamics, geopolitical developments, commodity markets, and increasingly, the digital asset markets that AsterNews covers in parallel. Understanding the dollar's trajectory requires understanding Fed policy; understanding EUR/USD requires understanding the divergence between the ECB and the Fed; and understanding the future of currency in the digital age increasingly requires understanding stablecoins and central bank digital currencies.
